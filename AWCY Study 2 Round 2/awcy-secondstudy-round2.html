User Study: Andy

Stanford University is developing an open source privacy-protecting virtual assistant, called Andy. We'd like to know how interested you would be in using Andy.

How concerned are you about privacy of smart assistants such as Alexa or Google Assistant? On a scale of 1-4, where:

  1. Not at all concerned
  2. A little concerned
  3. Concerned, but that’s the way it is. I will use it if it is useful.
  4. I’m so concerned about privacy that I will not use it even if I find it useful.

Instructions
